# Welcome to Nature's Journey: Beyond the Horizon!

The eagerly awaited sequel to the popular Minecraft modpack that takes exploration, combat, and kingdom-building to new heights.

In Nature's Journey: Beyond the Horizon, the focus is on pushing the boundaries of creativity and gameplay. From awe-inspiring aesthetics to groundbreaking automation, get ready to explore uncharted territories, engage in thrilling combat encounters, and establish flourishing colonies and kingdoms.

Nature's Journey: Beyond the Horizon is more than a modpack; it's a gateway to boundless creativity, challenging exploration, and epic kingdom-building. Are you ready to embark on the next chapter of your Minecraft journey? The world awaits your exploration and conquest.

## Key Features

Unleash your creativity with the powerful [Create Mod](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/create), allowing you to build intricate and visually stunning automated systems. From conveyor belts to rotating contraptions, automation has never been this aesthetically pleasing.

Traverse your world like never before with mods like [Immersive Aircraft](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/immersive-aircraft) and [Small Ships](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/small-ships)! Construct mighty ships and navigate through treacherous waters, discovering hidden islands and treasures along the way or take to the skies with new flying machines, allowing you to soar above your world and marvel at its beauty. Whether it's majestic airships or small cogs, the world is yours to conquer.

Reach for the stars and venture into space with [Ad Astra](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ad-astra). Establish extraterrestrial colonies, mine on other planets, and unlock the mysteries of the cosmos.

Encounter a variety of new creatures and explore diverse biomes that will challenge your survival skills. From mythical beasts to exotic landscapes, Nature's Journey: Beyond the Horizon adds a plethora of content to keep your adventures fresh and exciting.

Nature's Journey: Beyond the Horizon includes lots of performance-enhancing mods, ensuring smooth and lag-free adventures. Dive into the modpack without compromising on performance, even with the added complexity of new features.
